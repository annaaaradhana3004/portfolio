Case Study
Spatchcock Funk —
A Growth Strategy

The Challenge

Spatchcock Funk, an independent food & beverage media brand, needed a scalable, data-driven strategy to grow their audience, drive revenue, and identify the right technology to unify their marketing efforts.

Our Approach

Conducted audience segmentation, identifying two core personas — Sophisticated Sam (45, Denver professional) and Creative Cara (28, Brooklyn creative). We mapped behavior, platform habits, and spending patterns to build a unified strategy.

The Recommendation

Recommended a Twilio Segment + Salesforce tech stack deployed across four phases over 18 months — from platform launch and A/B testing to live event activation and predictive sponsorship modeling.

Twilio SegmentSalesforce CRMA/B TestingPersona StrategyLive Events

Outcome

Strategy projected to generate $151K in incremental revenue and +15% audience growth YoY. Team placed 3rd at the Whitman MBA Case Competition.
